Implementing Wind Tree Turbines

In November of 2019, I worked on a consulting project with Alphabet Inc's subsidary Sidewalk Labs in order to find a way to decrease cost of living by 40% below market value. Working alongside my team, we analyzed energy costs a Sidewalk's Proposed Energy plan. From this, we took a look at more unconventional sources of energy. What we ended up pitching was Vertical-Axis 'Wind-Tree' Turbines. In a nutshell, these are quiet, domestic, wind turbines, that provide smaller amounts of energy in exchange for being more accessible. You can check our recommendation here!
