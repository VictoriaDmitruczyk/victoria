Kidogo is a company that aims to bring high quality education to families with low income, as a form of cost-effective and accessible private schooling. In March of 2020, I had the great opportunity to work on a problem statement for them, in which our aim was to increase the profit of their caregivers, known as 'mampreneurs'. My team was able to increase revenue by 258%, increasing the income of the caregivers while also increasing the quality of the centres! I'm really grateful to have had the opportunity to make an impact in the education space, a topic which I'm really passionate about. Check out our recommendation here!
