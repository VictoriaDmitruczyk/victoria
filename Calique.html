3.5 billion people every year are at risk of developing Calcium deficiency. While this problem may sound insignificant, it leads to problems like osteoporosis, cardiac arrest, and even dementia in the future. However, many individuals do not even know what their calcium levels are, and do not care. In order to obtain this number, you need to go to a doctor, who invasively checks it. What if there was a way to have live time insights and be constantly monitoring this number in order to prevent those adverse side effects? Calique is a project in which your Calcium and pH+ levels can be non-invasively monitored via an integration with a smart watch, applying the Internet of Things.
