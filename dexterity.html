Currently 74% of people live in multidimensional poverty. Individuals stuck in these situations often go through Vicious Cycles, where one mistake can trigger a series of events that have the potential to be catastrophic to one's life. Education is the first step in breaking out of this vicious cycle, however in a lot of places, especially developing countries, education is expensive and inaccessible. Why should someone travel two hours for school when they can work a job and bring in income? Many individuals are faced with these hard decisions every day. The mission of dexterity is to bring education to people. By using Raspberry Pi's and cost-efficient LED screens which have downloaded content on them, individuals can access education in an easy and financially accessible way. Dexterity specifically provides content on five main pillars; sanitation, medicine, personal security, food and water security, add education.
